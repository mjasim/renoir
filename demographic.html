Color of Emotions
About

Welcome to the color of emotion survey. Emotions are a crucial component of human communication that helps people to express their feelings. However, in online text-based discussions, this indicator is missing, which leads to an incomplete understanding of the discussions. Research shows an association between emotions and colors. We posit colors can help express emotions captured from text data when used appropriately in visualization. In this survey, we want to learn how you associate colors with different emotions. Our goal is to identify a representative color palette to visualize emotions from online discussion text. The survey will take about 10 minutes. Thank you for participating in the survey.
This study was reviewed and approved by the University of Massachusets Amherst Institutional Review Board
